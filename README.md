# ProjexHub

**Author:** Saniya Bhagat

**ProjexHub** is your all-in-one **Project & Task Management Tool**  
designed to simplify how teams and individuals plan, track, and deliver projects.

ProjexHub offers a centralized space to plan, organize, track, and complete work efficiently.  

Whether you're managing a personal side project or collaborating across teams,  
ProjexHub adapts to your needs with flexible features and real-time updates.
